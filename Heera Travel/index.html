About Us

Offering car rental services, Heera Travels in Andheri East, Mumbai is a name to be reckoned with.

Since 2000, We are in the business of offering cars on rent. Our venture aims to offer individuals as well as corporates with superlative car hire services. Our establishment has provided impeccable services to a large number of customers. Our professionally managed organization believes in offering quality services at reasonable price points.

Services

We offer a wide choice in terms of their fleet which features 4 x 4, SUVs as well as Luxury Cars. Take your pick as per your preferences. The fleet is well-maintained and kept in excellent condition for the passengers' comfort and convenience. We also offer long term rentals and fleet management services.

Local and Transport

We provide cars as per the customer requirement. Our packages are categorized as per their need. For Local Use : Our minimum package is 8 hours & 80 kilometers in which you can use the cab for 8 hours up to 80kms. Extra kilometers ,extra hours, Toll & Parking will be charged extra. For Transfer Use: Our minimum package is 4 hours & 40 kilometers in which you can use cab for airport pickups or airport drops within 4 hours and 40 kilometers. Note: Kilometers and Hours are calculated from Garage to Garage. For more T&C* & Pricing please refer Pg.13,14.

OutStation

We also provide for people/customers who want to travel for fun vacation or for interstate business meetings or trips. For Outstation Use: The package will be of 250 kms per day. In this, Toll & Parking, Driver Allowance/Day and Night Halt will be charged extra. Note: Please read the Terms & Condition for more simplified description Pg. 14.
